Additional Notes:

Please allow up to 24 hours for order processing. Orders are shipped out Monday thru Friday.

Canadian customers pay GST of 5% at checkout. Prices are listed in CAD, and all orders are processed in CAD at checkout.

All orders are shipped from our warehouse in Calgary, Alberta.

For US & international orders, duties, taxes and customs fees are the responsibility of the customer.

HEMM takes great care to ensure your shipment arrives on time. However, we cannot be held liable for delayed or lost shipments. Contact us at sales@hemm.ca and we will assist you in anyway we can.

Orders under $150 are shipped without a signature. Orders over $150 are shipped with a signature required upon delivery.

Additional Notes:

All items being returned have to be in mint condition, with all tags, original packaging and in brand new condition. Please take care when trying on your items at home. Undies are a must!

All sale items and items purchased with a discount code (excluding the use of the "10% off your first purchase" offer) are a final sale and will not be eligible for a price adjustment, return or an exchange.

Non-returnable items: yoga mats, yoga towels, jewelry and socks are final sale & cannot be returned. Gift cards are non-returnable and non-refundable.
